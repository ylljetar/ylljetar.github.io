How does Artish work?

Download Artish to your mobile to get started. It is completely free! Secondly you have to create an account to sell and buy the art. You can then search and browse through thousands of great posts to discover what type of art you are interested in. Follow accounts you like the art style of and like posts that capture your attention. Have you discovered something you would like to purchase? Chat directly with the seller to ask questions about the art and arrange how you want it delivered or place a bid on the product. To sell your art you can post how many posts you would like and customize your own feed.
